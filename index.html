About Me

Hello, I’m Olleh, a freelance videographer and photographer with a passion for visual storytelling. With three years of hands-on experience in professional video and photo production, I specialize in creating high-quality visual content that captures attention and delivers results. My journey in production has equipped me with a versatile skill set, ranging from pre-production planning and lighting to shooting and advanced post-production editing.
